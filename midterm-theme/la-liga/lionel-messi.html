Lionel Messi, the little man from Argentina that had one of the greatest if not the greatest impact in football history. Messi is dubbed the best player to play the beautiful game over the likes of Cristinao Ronaldo, Maradona, Pele, Zidane, etc. Obviously, for me he is at the top of the list as well. Messi is my favorite player to play football and I am honored to be able to grow up watching him play. It was the start of a new era. To be able to have him compete as well as against Cristiano Ronaldo is amazing. In my opinion he is the most fun to watch because of his amazing dribbling and vision down the field.

Start of his career

You can say it was rough for Messi at the start. From a very young age Messi was diagnosed with a growth hormone deficiency. But that did not stop Messi. His first club was Newell's Old Boys but later on joined F.C Barcelona's under age 14s team at the age of 13. At the age of 17 Lionel Messi made his first team debut for F.C Barcelona marking the start of an era. His first goal came by an assist of the likes of Ronaldinho himself. One of the greatest of all time and one who made an arguement as the best player in the world at one point.

World Records

When we talk about world records, Messi holds a lot of the records for Football. Excelling from a young age has aid Messi to be able to accomplish some of these records.

To name a few, Messi has the most FIFA World Player of the Year awards with 6, Shares the most Ballon d'Or awards with Cristiano Ronaldo at the count of 5, Guinness World Record with the most oficial goals in a calender year with a shocking 91 goals which he broke in 2012, Messi is the only player in history to be able to score 40 or more club goals in 9 consecutive seasons, shares the most appearances in the top 3 candidates for the Ballon d'Or along with Cristiano Ronaldo, Messi is the youngest player to win 2, 3, 4, and 5 ballon d'Or awards, has the most league goals accross all of Europes top 5 leagues in hisotry with 423 goals, most league goals for a singal club at 423 goals, youngest player ever to score 400 or more goals for a singal club achieving this record at the age of 27, and holds the record for the most official goals for a club in Europes top 5 leagues with 608 goals. This are jsut some of his achieves, yes this is not an understatement. Overall, this man is just amazing.
